The Great Science Quiz

1. What is the process by which plants make their own food?

Options: photosynthesis, digestion




2. Which of the following is a physical change?
Options: melting ice, burning paper



3. What is the main function of the circulatory system?
Options: to transport oxygen and nutrients, to digest food



4. Which state of matter has a definite shape and volume?
Options: solid, gas



5. What is the force that pulls objects toward the Earth?
Options: gravity, magnetism



6. Which of the following is a non-renewable resource?
Options: coal, wind energy



7. What is the main function of the root of a plant?
Options: to make food,to absorb water



8. Which of the following is an example of a chemical change?
Options: water evaporating, iron rusting



9. What does the water cycle include?
Options: Melting, Evaporation
